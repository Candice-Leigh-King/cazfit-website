Hey, I'm Caz!

I'm the owner, founder and face of CazFit, and it's so nice to meet you.

To me personal training is more than just the training itself. It is about establishing healthy relationships with my clients and being part of their journey. It brings me so much joy seeing my clients enjoy their exercise sessions. Ontop of personal training sessions and FUNctional group exercise sessions, I also specialize in safe exercise for seniors, pregnant women and children. I also work as a group exercise instructor at Virgin Active and I am co owner of FitTogether.
